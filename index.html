Welcome to CNC Proto!

An online CNC machining service in Europe for rapid prototyping and low-volume production of small to medium sized parts in aluminium and engineering plastics.

CNC Proto is the perfect partner to bring your concepts to life. Explore our gallery of Sample parts to get a feel for what CNC Proto can do. If you like what you see, check the Services page to learn more about what we can offer you or fill out the Quote request form to get started.

We’re engaged to deliver your CNC machined parts on time, on tolerance and on budget. Always.

How can we help you

We specialise in rapid prototyping and low-volume production (1 to 100+) of end-use components in aluminium and engineering plastics using modern CNC machines.

With manufacturing facilities located close to Lisbon, Portugal, we can deliver high-quality precision CNC machined parts to the European market with short turnaround times.

While operating our own workshop, we strive to offer the highest quality standards on everything we produce. As such, in order to have full control over the entire production of your parts, we do everything in-house and do not outsource any process to third-party suppliers.

We provide precision CNC machined parts for a wide range of fields, ranging from the medical, railway and automotive industries, to the aeronautical, aerospace and R&D sectors. We also cater to the fast-expanding market of makers and hobbyists.

A focus on fast turnaround precise parts

- Precision CNC Milling and Turning

We offer precision 3+1 axis milling and 2 axis turning services for small to medium sized parts. These capabilities combined with our engineering knowledge, skill and expertise ensures your parts are produced exactly as you need them.

- Specialist in Aluminium and Engineering Plastics

We specialise in machining parts out of non-ferrous metals (mostly 6000 and 7000 series aluminium alloys) and engineering plastics (POM-C, UHMW-PE, PEEK, etc.). Of course, if your project contains the odd part in a material other than these, we will do our best to accomodate your request.

- No Minimum Quantity

Tired of long production lead times or high part quantity requirements? We know the feeling. No matter how small your quantity needs are, we can accommodate your project and deliver prototype CNC machined parts in a matter of days.

- Quote in 24H

In the modern world, speed is key. We will respond to your quote request within 24 hours, verifying your requirements and giving you a competitive offer.
