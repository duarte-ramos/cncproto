Quote request

It will only take a minute...

Please fill the form below by adding your name, address, email, business name and VAT ID (if any), etc.. This information will be used to respond with an all inclusive email quotation for your CNC machined parts. We respect your privacy and will not use the information you provide us for marketing purposes, nor will we sell it to third parties.

Specify the desired part quantity, material, finish and general tolerances and attach your 3D model files (.STEP, .STP). Reference 2D drawings are not strictly required in our process though always helpful if you have specific design criteria that you are trying to accomplish. Attaching additional reference drawings can only help (.DXF, .DWG, .PDF). If any special instructions or comments need to be relayed (for example threaded or reamed holes), please include them also.

Provided that no additional information is required, you will receive your quote within the next 24 hours. If there are any concerns or questions on your parts we will contact you via email.

Please note that we can only reply to requests from countries within the European Union.
